Why AMI Auto Spa?

Washing your car at home may be the best way if you have time and just need a little cleaning off the car's surface. Full service car washes provide special ingredients that clean dust, residues, and that offer rust prevention for your vehicle. In general, we recommend washing about twice a month and more frequently during the winter, with an application of a wax and under wash at least every other wash. This will add a protective shine to your car's finish and keep it looking like new. We offer special window and glass waxes to protect the car from wear and tear as well as provide water resistance to rain especially in the front window areas.
